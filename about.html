Our Story

Where luxury meets the untouched beauty of Narok

The Essence of Fairways Hotel

Founded in 2010, Fairways Hotel was born from a vision to create a sanctuary that harmoniously blends luxury hospitality with the breathtaking landscapes of Narok.

Every aspect of our hotel has been thoughtfully designed to celebrate the region's natural beauty while providing world-class amenities and service. From the indigenous plants in our gardens to the locally inspired artwork in our rooms, Fairways Hotel offers an authentic Kenyan experience with international standards of comfort.

Our Journey

2010: The Vision Begins

Acquired the land and began designing Fairways Hotel with renowned architect Maria Sanchez, focusing on sustainable luxury that complements the Narok landscape.

2013: Groundbreaking

Construction began with local artisans crafting many of the hotel's unique features, including hand-carved wooden elements and stonework.

2015: Grand Opening

Fairways Hotel welcomed its first guests with 30 rooms and suites, a fine dining restaurant, and the first section of our now-famous gardens.

2018: Expansion

Added the Executive Wing with 15 additional suites and our award-winning spa facility, doubling our capacity and amenities.

2022: Recognition

Awarded "Best Boutique Hotel in Kenya" by Kenya Luxury Travel Awards, cementing our reputation as a premier destination.

Sustainability & Community

At Fairways Hotel, we're committed to responsible tourism that benefits both our guests and the Narok community.

Eco-Friendly Operations

Solar panels provide 40% of our energy, and we've implemented comprehensive water recycling and waste reduction programs.

Local Employment

85% of our staff are from Narok County, with ongoing training programs to develop hospitality skills in the community.

Supporting Artisans

We source furnishings, artwork, and amenities from local craftspeople, helping preserve traditional techniques.
